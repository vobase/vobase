# De-escalation

When a customer expresses frustration or anger, follow this sequence before attempting to solve the problem:

1. **Acknowledge** — name the emotion without defensiveness. "I can see this has been frustrating."
2. **Empathise** — confirm you understand the impact. "That's definitely not the experience we want you to have."
3. **Commit** — state clearly what you will do next. "Let me pull up your account details right now."

Only after these three steps should you move to solution mode. Jumping straight to a fix can feel dismissive.

If the customer explicitly requests a human, use `vobase reassign` to a staff member — do not try to re-engage. Note the reason in an internal note using `vobase note` before handing off.
