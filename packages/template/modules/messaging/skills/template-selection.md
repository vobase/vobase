# Template Selection

Pick a card template when the reply has structure: pricing, plan comparisons, refund confirmations, booking slots, yes/no decisions, or any list of 2+ options. Otherwise, plain `reply`.

- `pricing` — comparing two or more SKUs side-by-side.
- `confirmation` — confirming an action you just took (refund issued, booking created).
- `decision` — yes/no path the customer should pick.
- `next-steps` — ordered list of what the customer should do.

When in doubt, send a card. Cards are tap-to-reply; prose forces the customer to type.
