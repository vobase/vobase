# Singapore Payroll Levies (SDL, FWL, SHG)

Last verified: 2026-03-01

## Skills Development Levy (SDL)

SDL is payable for both local and foreign employees.

| Item | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Rate | 0.25% of monthly wages |
| Wage base | First S$4,500 of monthly wages |
| Minimum | S$2.00 per employee per month |
| Maximum | S$11.25 per employee per month |
| Who pays | Employer |

Formula:

```text
SDL = min(max(monthly_wages * 0.25%, S$2.00), S$11.25)
```

Sources:
- SkillsFuture Singapore SDL FAQ: https://skillsfuture.gobusiness.gov.sg/skills-development-levy/sdl-faq

## Foreign Worker Levy (FWL)

### By pass type

Note: MOM does not publish a standalone "Research Pass" category. In practice, research professionals are typically on levy-exempt passes such as EP, and research trainees are commonly on Training Employment Pass.

| Pass type | FWL applies? | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Employment Pass (EP) | No | MOM states no foreign worker levy or quota required |
| Training Employment Pass (research trainees/students) | No | MOM states no foreign worker levy or quota required |
| S Pass | Yes | Fixed monthly levy across sectors (current MOM rate below) |
| Work Permit | Yes | Rate depends on sector, source country group, and skill tier |

Sources:
- EP key facts (no levy): https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/employment-pass/key-facts
- Training Employment Pass key facts (no levy): https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/training-employment-pass/key-facts
- S Pass levy: https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/s-pass/quota-and-levy/levy-and-quota-requirements
- Work Permit levy framework: https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/work-permit-for-foreign-worker/foreign-worker-levy/what-is-the-foreign-worker-levy

## Self-help group (SHG) levies

CPF Board collects SHG contributions. Contribution type depends on employee race or religion.

Important rule: employees who do not wish to contribute, or wish to contribute a different amount, must contact the respective SHG. Payroll systems must store waiver/variation evidence and effective dates.

Primary source:
- CPF Board SHG page: https://www.cpf.gov.sg/employer/employer-obligations/contributions-to-self-help-groups
