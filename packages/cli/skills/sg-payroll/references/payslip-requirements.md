# MOM Itemised Payslip Requirements

Last verified: 2026-03-01

## Issuance timing and format

| Rule | MOM requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Regular payroll run | Give payslip together with salary payment |
| If not given at payment time | Must be issued within 3 working days of payment |
| Termination/dismissal | Give payslip with outstanding salary |
| Allowed format | Soft copy or hard copy (including handwritten) |

Operational note: soft copy includes email attachments, PDF portals, and digital HR systems if employees can access item details.

Source:
- MOM itemised payslips: https://www.mom.gov.sg/employment-practices/salary/itemised-payslips

## Mandatory fields (MOM item list)

MOM requires the items below unless not applicable (for example, overtime fields when no overtime exists).

| # | MOM item | Implementation guidance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Full name of employer | Store employer legal name; include UEN in payroll system metadata for audit traceability |
| 2 | Full name of employee | Include employee identifier (NRIC/FIN/staff ID) in system record for reconciliation |
| 3 | Date of payment | Use actual payment date; include all dates if consolidated payslip |
| 4 | Basic salary | For hourly/daily/piece-rated staff, include basic rate and total units worked |
| 5 | Start and end date of salary period | Keep exact period boundaries used for payroll computation |
| 6 | Allowances paid | Break down fixed and ad-hoc allowances |
| 7 | Additional payments | Break out bonuses, rest day pay, public holiday pay, and similar components |
| 8 | Deductions made | Break down fixed and ad-hoc deductions (for example CPF employee share, SHG deduction, no-pay leave deductions) |
| 9 | Overtime hours worked | Required where overtime applies |
| 10 | Overtime pay | Required where overtime applies |
| 11 | Start and end date of overtime period | Required if overtime period differs from salary period |
| 12 | Net salary paid in total | Net amount after all deductions |

Source:
- MOM item list: https://www.mom.gov.sg/employment-practices/salary/itemised-payslips

## Salary record retention

| Employee status | Retention requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Current employees | Latest 2 years of pay slip records |
| Ex-employees | Last 2 years of records, retained for 1 year after leaving |

Source:
- MOM salary records guidance: https://www.mom.gov.sg/employment-practices/salary/itemised-payslips
